Prevention Checklist

## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always verify the sender before responding  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Never share OTP or bank details with anyone  
- Regularly update your devices and software  
- Use trusted websites for online transactions  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering details  
- For financial fraud, immediately report to cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call helpline number 1930 in case of online fraud
